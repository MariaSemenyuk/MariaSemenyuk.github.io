Target Audience

Audience: An audience of this website is Idaho adults citizen who lived in these towns and tourists who planned to visit cultural and natural tours at these three places.

Age: 16 - 80.

Technology: They used mobile phones mostly, and some of them used desktops.

Motivation: To be ready for the coming weather. To plan an activity outside or visit nature.

Income: $60 000 - $120 000

Personas:

Scenarios

  • What is the weather on Saturday in Fish Haven?
  • What can I see interesting at Soda Springs?
  • I have a meeting in one hour. What weather for this time?
  • I planned to visit an old house in Preston to prepare for the sale and make a few photos. Is there raining today?
  • We will go to the park with the children. Do we need to take hats?
  • There is rain. How long it will be?
