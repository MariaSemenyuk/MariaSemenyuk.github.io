Welcome to the Älvsjö Chamber of Commerce!
Since 1992, we have promote the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business and people to our district. Chamber membership provides a networking and knowledge sharing platform for members of the district business community to meet, connect, and learn. To that end we host numerous activities at which members can connect with other executives, business leaders, and government officials. Membership also provides an effective way to expose employees to the international business community, thus enhancing their professional development.
